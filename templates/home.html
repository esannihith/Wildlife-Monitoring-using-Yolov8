WILDGUARD

Wildlife Monitoring & Intrusion Detection System

About Our Project

WildGuard is an innovative wildlife monitoring and poacher detection system that leverages the power of YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art AI model. Our system provides real-time detection and monitoring capabilities to protect wildlife in their natural habitats.

By implementing advanced computer vision algorithms, WildGuard can identify both wildlife and potential poaching threats, enabling quick response times and better protection of endangered species.
